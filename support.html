Frequently Asked Questions

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Open your iPhone Settings > tap your name at the top > Subscriptions > CareFind. From there you can change your plan or cancel. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period.

Is CareFind a medical service?

No. CareFind is a healthcare provider discovery tool for informational purposes only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for medical concerns.

How are CareFind Scores calculated?

CareFind Scores are based on publicly available information including patient reviews, board certifications, experience, and other quality indicators. Scores are for informational purposes only and do not guarantee provider quality.

Is my health data secure?

Yes. Health journal entries, symptom data, and appointment information are encrypted on your device using AES-256-GCM encryption with keys stored in the iOS Keychain. Your health data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Profile > Account > Delete Account. This will remove all your data. If you have an active subscription, make sure to cancel it separately through your Apple ID settings.

How do I restore my subscription on a new device?

Open CareFind on your new device, go to Profile > Manage Subscription > Restore Purchases. Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID and will be restored automatically.
